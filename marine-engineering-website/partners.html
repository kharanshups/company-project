RacMet Partnership

Strategic Alliance with RacMet

Marine Engineering & Shipbuilding is proud to announce our strategic partnership with RacMet, a globally recognized leader in advanced marine materials and metallurgical solutions. This partnership represents a significant milestone in our commitment to providing cutting-edge shipbuilding capabilities.

Partnership Benefits

  • Advanced Materials: Access to RacMet's premium marine-grade alloys and composites
  • Quality Assurance: Rigorous testing and certification processes for all materials
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly materials aligned with modern environmental standards
  • Cost Efficiency: Optimized material sourcing reducing overall project costs
  • Technical Support: Expert consultation on material selection and application
  • Innovation: Joint R&D initiatives for next-generation marine materials

About RacMet

RacMet is a leading international supplier of specialized metals and metallurgical services. With facilities across Europe and Asia, they serve the most demanding applications in aerospace, marine, and industrial sectors.
