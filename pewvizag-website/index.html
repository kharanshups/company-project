About PEW Vizag

PEW Vizag stands as one of India's foremost ship construction and repair enterprises, headquartered in the vibrant port city of Visakhapatnam — the maritime gateway of Eastern India.

With decades of unparalleled expertise, we have built a legacy defined by unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and engineering excellence. Our state-of-the-art shipyard facilities are equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by a highly skilled workforce dedicated to delivering superior maritime solutions.

From crafting purpose-built commercial vessels to executing complex repair and refurbishment projects, PEW Vizag operates with precision and passion. Our integrated approach, combining advanced design capabilities with robust production infrastructure, enables us to handle projects of any scale — from small fishing trawlers to large ocean-going cargo ships.

We adhere to the highest international standards, ensuring every vessel leaving our yard meets rigorous classification society requirements. Our quality management systems are certified and regularly audited, reflecting our relentless pursuit of excellence in every weld, every hull, and every sea trial.
